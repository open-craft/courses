Assessment Description

In this assessment, you will apply what you have learned about networking so far and grow your network using the MASTER model.

Objectives:

  • Implement best practices for networking in person and online

  • Apply the MASTER model to grow your personal network

  • Identify the challenges faced while applying the model

Instructions

1. Review the MASTER model. Following the MASTER model instructions, make a comprehensive list of your contacts—both personal and professional—who can contribute to your professional growth, both directly and indirectly. List 20 to 30 people.
