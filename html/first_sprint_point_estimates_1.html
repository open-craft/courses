Please participate in the grooming session (point estimation) that the team does at the end of each sprint. Usually you should get an email about this and can follow the link to reach the estimation page, but if not, try this link to see if you have been invited to any session. When grooming, you don't have to provide a number of points for every issue (or even most) - put '?' if you don't feel you have enough knowledge to estimate a task.
