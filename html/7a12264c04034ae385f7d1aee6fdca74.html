Our Engage | Network | Progress Learner Journey helps students not only complete their program of study but also empowers graduates to earn a return on their tuition investment. This is done via industry relevant networking and career progression modules that connect the learning to employment or further study goals.

A key part of our value proposition is the sense of community that we bring to the often isolating experience that is common in traditional, open online courses. Each program milestone that we’ve added has been thoughtfully designed to help students grow and develop. Here’s how we do it:

  • ENGAGE with the content through interactivities, live meetings, and discussions with peers and faculty

  • NETWORK with thought leaders, alumni and peers in the field of study

  • PROGRESS to your end goal via a Student Success Advisor relationship, our Career Progression Series and our digital badging system that provides a shareable credential upon successful program completion
